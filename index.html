Subway of Budapest

Symbols

  • Each metro line’s starting station is outlined in its respective line color.
  • Black squares ( ■ ) represent railway stations.

How to draw a segment

  • You always select in this order: FROM → TO.
  • First click: choose the FROM station.
  • Second click: choose the TO station.
  • The selected FROM station is highlighted by a orange outline.
  • The selected TO station is highlighted with a light-blue outline.
  • You can cancel selections by cliking the stations again, but only in the order TO → FROM You cannot clear FROM while a TO is still selected.
  • Press "Confirm Segment" button to draw a line.
